Cancellation and Refund policy

If You Decide Not To Attend The Training Workshop After Having Registered, We May Be Able To Cancel Your Registration And Refund Your Payment Depending On Our Refund Policies. Important Provisions Of Our Refund Policy Are Provided Below.

No refund or cancellation will be possible if a request is made less than 24 hours before commencement of the training workshop.
  • Request for refund should be made by sending an email to deepak.alok@m2iconsulting.com
  • The person filling up the registration form authorizes M2i to send registration, enrollment or course related communication to him/her on the provided email ID and the phone number.

  • No partial refund will be possible.
  • In case you are unable to attend the training, you can nominate someone else from your own organisation to participate.
  • Once a request for refund is made, the amount will be refunded within 7 days.
