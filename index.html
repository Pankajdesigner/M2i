Course objective

Financial sustainability is one of the most important objectives of Microfinance Institutions (MFIs). The MFIs need to manage multiple facets of performance to achieve financial sustainability. This training workshop on Financial Analysis of MFIs will expose participants to tools and benchmarks used to measure Financial performance of the MFIs. The training will also be useful for the investors, donors and lenders who want to assess performance of the MFIs.

Delivery methodology

The training is organized in a ‘Live Virtual’ format over three days. The trainers and participants interact over Zoom Video calls. Case studies, presentations, exercises and games are used for effective assimilation of concepts.

Participants will get access to ‘M2i Learn’ mobile and web application during the training workshop. The app has have videos, e-books, games and quizzes, relevant to the training. The content on M2i Trainings mobile and web application will be available to the participants even after completion of the training.

Participants will need to have high speed internet connection to support video calls and a desktop or a laptop computer. Language of instruction will be English

Certificates will be issued to the participants once they successfully complete quizzes and assignments.

Who will benefit?

  • Senior and middle management personnel from MFIs
  • Bankers and investors
  • Academicians
  • Analysts and consultants
  • Undergraduate and postgraduate students

The course does not require any prior background in accounting and finance. However, basic knowledge of how Microfinance functions will be useful.
